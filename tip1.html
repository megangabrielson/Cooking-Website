Tip #1: Getting Spicy!


Spicy?!

Okay, so to be completely honest with you, I can't actually handle that much spice. Well, except Indian food. I loooove Indian food! (Mmmmm... do I spy Tikka Masala)? I actually made this dish, and the key, I found out, was the key to most things in cooking: use spices. Spices bring flavor to the food you're eating. There's a reason adults don't eat baby food -- it's not seasoned! (Among other things). So buy yourself some common household seasonings. I promise you'll use them. And if you don't? Well, you should. Here's a list of some common ones. Obviously it depends on what kind of stuff you like to make, but I think this is a good starter kit for most cuisine:
  • Pepper
  • Salt
    • (I know, good ol' salt and pepper)
  • Garlic
    • (Don't breathe on anyone though, lol)
  • Paprika
  • Oregano, parsley, basil
    (or other italian seasoning blends you might like)
  • Red pepper
  • Steak seasoning rub
  • Cinnamon
I could go on and on, but this is a good starter. Try it out sometime soon! Your tastebuds will be happier for it.
